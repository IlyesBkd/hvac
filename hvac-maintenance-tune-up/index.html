FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about HVAC maintenance.

How often should I get an HVAC tune-up?
Most experts recommend twice per year ‚Äî once in spring (for AC) and once in fall (for heating). At minimum, once a year.
What does a tune-up include?
A full inspection of your AC, furnace, thermostat, filters, refrigerant levels, electrical connections, ductwork, and safety controls.
How long does a tune-up take?
A standard HVAC tune-up takes 45‚Äì90 minutes depending on your system type and any issues found.
Will a tune-up really save me money?
Yes. Regular maintenance can reduce energy bills by up to 25% and prevent breakdowns that cost hundreds or thousands to repair.
Do I need a tune-up if my system is new?
Yes. Even new systems benefit from annual maintenance. It also keeps your manufacturer warranty valid in most cases.
